FAQs and Delivery

Where do you deliver?

We deliver across the Klang Valley by our own courier, and to the rest of West Malaysia by insulated parcel. East Malaysia delivery is available for air plants and soil-free minis — larger potted plants travel poorly, so we keep them local.

How much is delivery?

Flat rate RM 15, free for orders over RM 250. Bulky floor-size plants within the Klang Valley may be quoted separately after checkout — we will always confirm with you before anything is charged.

How does payment work?

When you place an order, we record it and open WhatsApp with your order details ready to send to us. Once you send it, we reply to arrange payment — bank transfer, Touch 'n Go, or DuitNow — and confirm delivery before the plants leave the studio. No card details are entered on this site.

Will my plant look exactly like the photo?

Photos show a representative piece from the batch. Plants are living things — leaf count and shape vary a little, but size, pot and overall character will match what you see.

What if my plant arrives damaged?

Send us a photo within 48 hours of delivery and we will replace the plant or refund you. Slight travel stress (a bent leaf, a bit of dropped moss) usually recovers within a week.

Do you do custom or bulk arrangements?

Yes — corporate gifts, event tables, and custom soil-free arrangements are a big part of what we do. Write to us with your date and budget.

Can I self-collect?

Self-collection is available at our KL studio by appointment, and at any of our weekend pop-ups — choose the option in your order notes and we will coordinate.

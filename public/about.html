About Plantmood

Plantmood is a Kuala Lumpur plant studio built around one idea: every plant has a mood, and so does every home. Our job is to match the two — the right green for your light, your schedule, and the way you actually live.

Soil-free first

Our signature Soil-free Series raises real plants in a clean, soilless medium — no spills on the desk, no repotting anxiety, no fungus gnats. The same thinking runs through our hydroponic jars, where roots become part of the display, and our air plants, which ask for nothing but light and a weekly mist.

Arranged by hand

Every arrangement that leaves the studio is planted, styled and quality-checked by hand — from palm-size gift minis to floor-size statement greens. If a plant is not thriving, it does not ship. Simple as that.

Come say hi

You will find us at weekend pop-ups and markets around KL — bring your plant questions, or just come pat the moss. Follow @plantmood.my for dates.

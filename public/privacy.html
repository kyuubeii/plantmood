Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

When you place an order we collect your name, email, phone number and delivery address — the minimum needed to get plants to your door. If you subscribe to our newsletter we keep your email address, and if you message us we keep the conversation so we can follow up.

What we do with it

We use your details to fulfil orders, answer enquiries and — only if you subscribed — send occasional news about restocks and events. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Cookies & storage

Your shopping cart is stored locally in your own browser, not on our servers. We do not run third-party advertising trackers.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time, and you can ask us to view or delete the personal data we hold about you by writing to hello@plantmood.my. We comply with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (Malaysia).
